Muzzle-Flash
 
It is a 3D shooting game where your goal is to kill zombies with your weapon and not let the zombies attack you, otherwise you will die.
 
---
 
Gameplay & Objective
 
Kill zombies with your weapon and hide behind boxes until you've eliminated all of them.
 
Controls
 
W / A / S / D: Move
Mouse Right Click: Shoot
R: Reload
 
---
 
Features
 
Smooth and Responsive Movement: Fast and snappy mechanics designed for quick maneuvering around enemies.
 
Tactical Elements: Cover-based gameplay requiring strategic positioning behind obstacles to survive.
 
Dynamic Combat: Aiming, shooting, and reloading management loop under pressure.
 
---
 
Built With
 
Engine: [Godot Engine v4.x](https://godotengine.org/)
Language: GDScript
